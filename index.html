Jimmy Page

One of the best guitarists

Here is a time line of Jimmy Page's musical career

  • Early 1960's - Performed at the Marquee Club
  • 1965 - Hired by Stone manager as a producer
  • 1966 - joined the Yardbirds
  • 1968 - Led Zeppelin was formed
  • 1970's - Led Zeppelin toured and rocked
  • 1980 - The death of Bonham broke up the band
  • 1980's - Cameo music apparances and philanthropist work
  • 1994 - Page and Plant did MTV's "Unplugged" series
  • 2006 - Led Zeppelin inducted into the UK Music Hall of Fame
